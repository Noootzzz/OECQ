EDF - Quandela - Alice&Bob
CNRS|QET> - QEI#

At the heart of our project lies sustainable development, with the ambition to create energy-efficient quantum computers that offer a significant energy advantage over conventional computing systems. Alice&Bob and Quandela, in partnership with EDF, are committed to this first step towards environmentally friendly quantum computing.

Our scalable MNR methodology is designed to optimize the use of non-energy resources, underscoring our commitment to sustainable and responsible technology. Made in France, our project comes to life through local expertise: Alice&Bob exclusively manufactures its chips in France, while Quandela has inaugurated its first assembly plant in Massy. Our processors are shaped at CEA-Leti in Grenoble, and our photon sources come from C2N in Palaiseau.

The lifecycle management of quantum computers is at the forefront of our concerns. Energy optimization affects all computer components, reducing the material footprint and promoting a future where technology advances in tandem with the preservation of our planet.
